About This Book

Most writing prompts ask you what happens next. These ask you something harder.

They ask what a character carries in silence. What a scene refuses to say out loud. What a story means beneath the one being told.

The Deeper Story is a collection of fifty prompts designed specifically for literary fiction writers — writers who are not chasing plot twists, but truth. Writers who know that the most powerful sentences are the ones that arrive quietly, and stay.

Written by a retired English teacher with thirty years in the classroom and a lifelong devotion to literary fiction, each prompt in this collection is built to open a door, not close one. They are invitations into character, voice, memory, moral complexity, and the kind of storytelling that lingers long after the last page.

Whether you are working on a novel, a short story, or simply trying to find your way back to the page — these prompts will take you somewhere worth going.

A Taste of What's Inside
